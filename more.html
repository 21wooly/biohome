Construction Proccess

A lot goes into the construction of a biofriendly home. As part of our promise, we'll break down the proces and how we can be included to help lead the plan. 

The start of construction relies on two things: your vision, and your budget. We tailor our services so that construction is affordable for you, but also the finished product is admirable. Our experts find the property suited for you and wallet. Once location, budget, and happiness are assured, blueprints are made and it's time to work.    

The next stage is contracting and building. We cooperate with many companies to figure out the appliances needed in and around your house and divide up the work. We take pride in contracting experienced plumbers, electricians, and mechanics to assure the best of systems. Also in this stage, we cordinate with any Home Owner's Assosciations and insurance companies to solidify costs and the regulations after the build.  

The building process usually takes 4-6 months*
